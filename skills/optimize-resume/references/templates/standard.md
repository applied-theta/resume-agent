# Standard Resume Template

This template is used for the `linear_progression`, `entry_level`, `return_to_work`, and `international_candidate` archetypes. Section order varies by archetype -- see the Section Order Variants below.

---

## Template Structure

```markdown

## Professional Summary

[2-4 sentence summary following the pattern:]
[Professional identity] with [X] years of experience in [domain/specialization].
[Key achievement or capability with quantified impact].
[Value proposition or career focus statement].

## Technical Skills

**[Primary Category]:** [Skill 1], [Skill 2 (Acronym)], [Skill 3], ...
**[Secondary Category]:** [Skill 1], [Skill 2], ...
**[Tertiary Category]:** [Skill 1], [Skill 2], ...

## Professional Experience

### [Job Title]
**[Company Name]**, [City, State] | [Month YYYY] - [Present/Month YYYY]

- [Action verb] + [specific activity] + [measurable result with metrics]
- [Action verb] + [specific activity] + [measurable result with metrics]
- [Action verb] + [specific activity] + [measurable result with metrics]
- [3-6 bullets per role, most impactful first]

### [Previous Job Title]
**[Company Name]**, [City, State] | [Month YYYY] - [Month YYYY]

- [Action verb] + [specific activity] + [measurable result with metrics]
```

## Section Order Variants

### Linear Progression (Mid-Career, 3-10 Years)

1. Contact Information
2. Professional Summary
3. Technical Skills
4. Professional Experience (3-6 bullets per role, 2-4 roles)
5. Education
6. Certifications (if applicable)

**Emphasis:** Experience section is the centerpiece. Skills section shows breadth.
**De-emphasis:** Education is brief (degree, school, date only). Remove GPA unless exceptional (3.8+).
**Length:** 1-2 pages.

### Linear Progression (Senior, 10+ Years)

1. Contact Information
2. Professional Summary
3. Technical Skills / Core Competencies
4. Professional Experience (4-6 bullets for recent roles, 2-3 for older roles)
5. Education
6. Certifications (if applicable)
7. Speaking / Publications (if applicable)

**Emphasis:** Most recent 2 roles get the most detail. Show career progression and increasing scope.
**De-emphasis:** Roles older than 10-12 years can be condensed to title/company/dates with 1-2 bullets or omitted. Remove GPA entirely.
**Length:** 2 pages acceptable.

### Entry-Level (0-2 Years)

1. Contact Information
2. Summary / Objective
3. Education (prominent, include GPA if > 3.0, relevant coursework)
4. Projects (2-3 strong projects with technical details)
5. Experience (internships, part-time, relevant work)
6. Technical Skills
7. Activities / Leadership / Volunteer Work (if applicable)

**Emphasis:** Education and projects demonstrate capability. Coursework shows relevant knowledge.
**De-emphasis:** Limited experience is expected -- make the most of internships and projects. Avoid filler.
**Length:** 1 page strictly.

### Return-to-Work

1. Contact Information
2. Professional Summary (address career break positively, focus on current readiness)
3. Skills (updated with current technologies and recent training)
4. Recent Activity (contract work, freelance, volunteer, coursework during gap)
5. Previous Professional Experience
6. Education
7. Certifications / Recent Training

**Emphasis:** Recent skills and activities show currency. Summary addresses the gap without dwelling on it.
**De-emphasis:** Do not draw attention to gap years in the experience timeline. Focus forward, not backward.
**Length:** 1-2 pages.

### International Candidate

1. Contact Information (include visa/work authorization status if relevant)
2. Professional Summary (position global experience as a strength)
3. Technical Skills
4. Professional Experience (clarify company size/recognition for non-local employers)
5. Education (include degree equivalency notes if needed)
6. Certifications / Language Proficiency

**Emphasis:** Translate international experience into locally understood terms. Clarify company scale for unknown employers.
**De-emphasis:** Remove country-specific conventions that do not apply (photo, date of birth, marital status).
**Length:** 1-2 pages.

## Formatting Guidelines

- **Font:** Use clean, ATS-friendly fonts (Arial, Calibri, Garamond, or equivalent)
- **Margins:** 0.5-1 inch on all sides
- **Font size:** 10-12pt for body text, 12-14pt for name, 11-12pt for section headers
- **Line spacing:** 1.0-1.15
- **Bullet style:** Simple round bullets or hyphens; no custom characters
- **Consistency:** All experience entries must follow the same format pattern
- **Date format:** "Month YYYY" or "MM/YYYY" -- consistent throughout
- **No tables, text boxes, columns, or images** -- these break ATS parsing
- **No headers/footers with critical content** -- ATS systems often skip them
- **File format:** PDF (text-based) or Markdown
